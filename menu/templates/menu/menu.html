Savory Delights Bao Buns

Our fluffy bao buns are filled with a variety of savory ingredients, including braised pork belly, crispy chicken, and tofu with pickled vegetables. Each bite is a perfect blend of flavors and textures, bringing a taste of Asia to your plate.

Street Food Classics Korean BBQ Tacos

Our Korean BBQ tacos are a fusion of flavors, featuring marinated beef, spicy kimchi, and fresh cilantro, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. These tacos are a perfect example of how we blend traditional Asian flavors with modern street food concepts.

Fresh and Flavorful Vietnamese Pho

Our Vietnamese pho is a fragrant and flavorful noodle soup, made with a rich broth, tender slices of beef or chicken, fresh herbs, and rice noodles. It's a comforting and satisfying dish that showcases the best of Vietnamese cuisine.

Sweet Treats Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream

Our matcha green tea ice cream is a refreshing and creamy dessert, made with high-quality matcha powder. It's the perfect way to end your meal on a sweet note, with the unique and earthy flavor of matcha.
